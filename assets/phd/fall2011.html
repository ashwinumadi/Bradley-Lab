    • The broader Department of Energy Fellowship (2011/2012 competition is still listed as "pending FY 2011 federal appropriations decisions.)
    • National Defense Science & Engineering Graduate Fellowship (2011/2012 competition will open 1 September; no closing date noted. US nationals & citizens only).
    • IBM (2010/2011 deadline was 2 November; this year's version isn't posted yet. Please let me know if you see that change. Your advisor must nominate you for this one.)
    • The NSF's Graduate Research Fellowship Program (2010/2011 deadline was 18 November. Website currently says "application will be available later this summer.")
    • The Hertz Foundation (2011/2012 deadline: 31 October.)
    • The CIES, the organization that runs the Fulbright Scholar Program. (Deadline for "core" applications is 1 August each year.)
    • The Department of Energy's Computational Science Graduate Fellowship program (2010/2011 deadline was 11 January.)
  • Internships at Xerox PARC, Google, and NASA Ames
  • The Burroughs Wellcome Fund Travel Grant program, which funds travel to acquire new research techniques, to promote collaborations, and to attend courses. Eligibility: CS PhD students who are interested in investigating research opportunities in the biological sciences or incorporating their ideas and approaches into answering biological questions (2010/2011 deadline was 1 December.)
  • The National Science Foundation's (NSF) East Asia and Pacific Summer Institutes for U.S. Graduate Students (EAPSI) is "...a flagship international fellowship program for developing the next generation of globally engaged U.S. scientists and engineers knowledgeable about the Asian and Pacific regions" (2011/2012 deadline: 9 November)
  • NCAR's central webpage for grad-student opportunities
