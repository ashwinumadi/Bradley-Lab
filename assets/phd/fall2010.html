    • The National Science Foundation's (NSF) East Asia and Pacific Summer Institutes for U.S. Graduate Students (EAPSI) is "...a flagship international fellowship program for developing the next generation of globally engaged U.S. scientists and engineers knowledgeable about the Asian and Pacific regions" (2010/2011 deadline: 10 November)
